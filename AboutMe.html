Hello! I'm Hansuja Das!

I'm this website's creator. My hobbies are reading, painting, debating and coding! I have always enjoyed reading about inspiring women and I have even done a speech about it. Like I said in the Women's Day History section of this website, I really do believe that we as human beings have made progress in our fight to make the world a more equal place in terms of opportunities and rewards.


I hope that my website has thought you something new or made you feel better about yourself. The only thing that I ask from you ( the reader ) is to spread positivity to fellow women or men who need it. As Mother Teresa puts it, " Spread love everywhere you go. Let no one ever come to you without leaving happier. "
